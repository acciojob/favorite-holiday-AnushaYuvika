Manali

Manali is a beautiful hill station nestled in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush green forests. Known as the "Valley of the Gods," it is a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and culture. With the Beas River flowing through the town, Manali offers breathtaking views, pleasant weather, and a peaceful atmosphere.

  1. Solang Valley – Best for adventure sports like skiing and paragliding.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Famous for its snow-covered mountains and scenic beauty.
  3. Hidimba Devi Temple – An ancient temple surrounded by tall cedar trees.
  4. Jogini Waterfall – A peaceful spot ideal for short treks and nature lovers.
  5. Old Manali – Known for its cafes, shopping, and riverside charm.
  • Trout Fish – A local specialty served fresh from the Beas River.
  • Sidu – A traditional Himachali bread made with wheat flour.
  • Thukpa – A Tibetan noodle soup perfect for cold evenings.
  • Momos – Steamed or fried dumplings filled with spices and veggies.
  • Babru – A Himachali snack similar to kachori, stuffed with black gram paste.
